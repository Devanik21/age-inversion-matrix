Partial reprogramming for human age reversal needs chemistry that borrows the logic of Oct4, Sox2, Klf4 and c-Myc but avoids permanent pluripotency or oncogenic lock-in. The 20 below are conceptual scaffolds designed from first principles, not copied from existing drugs. Each aims for transient activation, built-in off-switches, and tissue-restricted action.

## Design logic
We treat each Yamanaka factor as a function to mimic, not a protein to overexpress. Oct4 and Sox2 are pioneer-cooperative DNA openers. Klf4 is a brake on senescence via p21 and TERT. c-Myc is a metabolic amplifier that must be time-limited. The molecules below use non-natural backbones, degradation timers, and epigenetic readers to keep activity to hours, not days.

## What to do next
These are paper concepts only. Any synthesis, cell testing, or animal work will require full validation by qualified molecular biologists, medicinal chemists, and institutional biosafety and ethics review. Do not attempt human use. If you take these to top labs, start with in vitro half-life, off-target transcriptomics, and cancer-safety assays before any age-reversal phenotyping.
